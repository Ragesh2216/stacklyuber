Our Core Values

Sustainability

Stackly is committing to becoming a fully electric, zero-emission platform by 2040, with 100% of rides taking place in zero-emission vehicles, on public transit, or with micro-mobility. We believe in building a greener future for every city we operate in.

Rides and Beyond

In addition to giving riders a way to get from point A to point B, we're working to bring the future closer with self-driving technology and urban air transport. Innovation is at the heart of our mission to redefine urban logistics.

Our Journey

2009

The beginning

Stackly is founded by Travis Kalanick and Garrett Camp after they couldn't find a cab in Paris.

2010

First ride

The first Stackly ride is requested in San Francisco, offering an exclusive black car service.

2012

StacklyX Launched

We introduced StacklyX, allowing people to provide rides using their own personal vehicles, revolutionizing the gig economy.

2014

Stackly Eats arrives

We expanded from moving people to moving food with the launch of our delivery service network.

2019

Going Public

Stackly becomes a publicly traded company on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker STACKLY.

Present

A global platform

Operating in thousands of cities worldwide, continuously innovating in logistics, delivery, and transit technologies.
