Frequently Asked Questions

I lost an item on my trip. What should I do?
The best way to retrieve an item you may have left in a vehicle is to call the driver. You can do this by going to Your Trips > selecting the trip > Find Lost Item > Contact driver about a lost item. If your driver doesn't answer, leave a voice message.
How do I report a safety incident?
If you felt unsafe during a trip, please let us know immediately. Go to Help > Trip Issues and Refunds > I had a safety issue. If you are in immediate danger, use the in-app Emergency Button to call local authorities.
Why was my card charged before my trip ended?
You may see a pending authorization hold on your payment method for the upfront price of your trip. This hold ensures there are funds available, but it is not a final charge. It will be voided if the trip is cancelled.
How do I change my payment method?
Open the app menu and tap 'Wallet' or 'Payment'. From there, you can tap 'Add Payment Method' to put a new card on file, or select an existing method to delete it or set it as your default.
